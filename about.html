MEET THE PARTY

MIKE WHEELER

The Heart of the Party

Mike is the one who never lets go. When Will disappeared, everyone felt fear — but Mike felt responsibility.

He believes in people even when they don’t believe in themselves. To Mike, friendship isn’t optional — it’s a promise.

Strength: Loyalty
Belief: Friends don’t leave.

DUSTIN HENDERSON

The Light

Dustin laughs when things get scary. Not because he isn’t afraid — but because he refuses to let fear win.

He brings warmth, humour, and hope. When the party feels broken, Dustin reminds them why they’re together.

Strength: Hope
Belief: There’s always light.

LUCAS SINCLAIR

The Voice of Truth

Lucas questions everything. He doesn’t follow blindly — he thinks, doubts, and challenges.

But when danger comes, he’s always the first to protect his friends. His honesty keeps the party grounded.

Strength: Courage
Belief: Truth matters.

WILL BYERS

The Reason

Will is where it all began. His disappearance turned friendship into a mission.

Sensitive and kind, Will reminds the party what they’re fighting for — each other.

Strength: Empathy
Belief: Belonging matters.

ELEVEN

The Power

Eleven grew up without a family — until she found The Party.

Her powers are strong, but her real strength is choosing love over fear.

Strength: Sacrifice
Belief: Family is chosen.

MAX MAYFIELD

The Strength

Max doesn’t follow rules — she makes her own.

Strong, fearless, and independent, she brings balance to the party and proves that strength can be quiet too.

Strength: Resilience
Belief: Keep going.
