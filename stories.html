THE HAWKINS POST

"Documenting the Impossible"

November 8, 1983

Boy Vanishes Into Thin Air, Friends Won't Accept It

Will Byers disappeared on November 6th. Just... gone. His bike found on the side of the road. No body. No trace.

The police called off the search after two days. His three best friends didn't listen.

Every night, Mike, Dustin, and Lucas rode their bikes calling his name until their voices went hoarse.

November 10, 1983

Lost Girl Found in Woods, Boys Take Her In Without Question

They found her in the rain. A girl. Shaved head. Wearing a hospital gown. Scared out of her mind.

Most kids would've run. These three brought her home. "She needed help. That's it. That's the whole reason."

The girl, who goes by "El," barely speaks. But these boys treat her like she's always been part of the group.

November 13, 1983

Missing Boy Found Alive, Rescue Led By Middle Schoolers

Will Byers is alive. He was found in the Upside Down, dark and cold. His friends rescued him, not the police.

But the rescue came at a cost. Eleven used every ounce of her strength to destroy the monster that had taken Will. She vanished in the process.

Mike won't talk about it. Just says, "She saved us. She saved Will. She's a hero."

November 1984

Boy Possessed By Entity, Friends Stay By His Side For Days

Will Byers can't catch a break. One year after the Upside Down, the "Mind Flayer" possessed him.

"He's in there. Fighting. We're not leaving until he comes back to us." Mike, Dustin, and Lucas stayed by him.

They saved him together, refusing to give up.

October 1984

New Girl Proves Herself, Group of Four Becomes Five

Max moved to Hawkins. Skateboarding, fierce, independent.

"She's not replacing anyone. She's just... Max. And we're better with her here." Max fought alongside them, proving herself.

December 1984

Eleven Returns, Closes Gate, Collapses

Eleven returned after a year. Immediately had to save everyone.

"For my friends. For Will. I had to." Mike held her while she cried from exhaustion.

Billy Hargrove died while protecting others. "He was terrible to me. He hurt me. But at the end... he saved me."

"She's fought for us a hundred times. We weren't letting her go."

They coordinated, executed, and saved her. She survived.

Present Day

Six Kids Who Survived Hell Together, Still Standing

Mike, Dustin, Lucas, Will, Eleven, Max. "We're The Party. We've been through hell. But we went through it together. That's why we're still standing."

Miles apart, trauma shared, bond unbreakable.

That's The Party. That's real friendship.
